Website Evaluations

Apple Website

This is Apple's website. They have a very clean modern design

  • Contrast

    I can read every word on this website. For the most part it is black font color on a white bakcground, but it works and is complemented with splashes of color for different products.

  • Repetition

    Most of the Headings are in a black font, with the descriptions being in grey which creates a uniform affect.

  • Alignment

    Products being displayed are in a box that aligns with the heading of what the info inside the boxes are. everything is aligned vertically, nothing is out of place or too big.

  • Proximity

    Products are labeled and grouped together in a sliding selection. Very nice and clean
